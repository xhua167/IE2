What can we do?

We are a group of 4 individuals collectively working to ease the way for domestic violence victims, especially female victims of the age 19-35 making the services more accessible more convenient. When you are in need of services, we provide the right available services which are free and nearby. These services include, accommodation, essential items, legal advice and a whole lot more. Finding those services more easily and making their life more convenient and bringing a ray of hope to their lives.
